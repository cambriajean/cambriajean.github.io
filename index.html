The Paris Review

Through the course of our relationship, I've discovered several (what I consider) classic movies that Paris has never seen. I started keeping a list. As we made our way through the list, I started asking her what she thought and the reviews were too good not to write down.

Who's Paris?

(why should you care about her reviews?)

Paris and I met in 2019, after she relocated to Seattle from Philly. At the time, her favorite movie was Ferris Bueller's Day Off. She brings a unique perspective to her movie reviews due to growing up in a hyper-critical, only-child, east-coast household (Paris' words). She was not allowed to have pets and now she is obsessed with my dog, Primrose. She would describe her passion for film as average.
